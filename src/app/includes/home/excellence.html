THE DUBAI MARITIME CITY AUTHORITY (DMCA)

DMCA aims to catapult Dubai into the ranks of the world’s most important and competitive maritime centers.

The evolution of Dubai’s maritime sector over the past five decades has been significant. in the continued spirit of progress, Dubai seeks to create a vibrant and safe maritime investment environment for industry leaders from all over the world, while reaffirming Dubai’s position as a leading international maritime hub. The Dubai Maritime City Authority (DMCA) was established to this end.
