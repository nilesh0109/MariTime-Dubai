THE LAUNCH OF THE DUBAI MARITIME CLUSTER OFFICE (DMCO)

DMCA established the Dubai Maritime Cluster Office in response to the Dubai government’s efforts to transform the local maritime sector into one of the world’s most comprehensive and competitive maritime clusters. The Office’s pivotal role is the strengthen the fruitful cooperation among government sector, private entities, and stakeholders involved in local, regional and global maritime activities to boost Dubai’s initiative to become a main global economic hub by 2021.
